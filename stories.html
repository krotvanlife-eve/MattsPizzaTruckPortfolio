Matt's Stories

Matt founded Tour De Pizza in 2008 on the principle that pizza is good food. Owner Matt McClellan biked from St. Petersburg to New York in 2010 to show that pizza can be healthy food and that you can lose weight eating it. He stopped at pizzerias along the way and appeared on TV to spread the gospel about pizza. He took some time off from the restaurant business. He is committed to providing customers with fresh, healthy food options at a reasonable price.
